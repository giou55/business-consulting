Strategy Statement

By leveraging our long shipping experience and increasingly cost-efficient operation, as well as, through carefully timed and negotiated acquisitions of modern medium-sized bulk carriers, we will grow and rejuvenate our fleet and provide our customers with distinctly reliable seaborne transportation services focused on the coal, grains, fertilizers and minerals trades, while generating superior returns for our shareholders and steadily increasing our footprint.

Vision, Mission & Core Values

Vision

To be recognised as a leading shipping company in the global seaborne transportation of coal, minerals, grains and fertilizers with an aggregate annual cargo volume lift of 10 million tonnes by 2020.

Mission

MissionTo provide distinctly reliable seaborne transportation services to shippers, receivers of cargo and vessel operators in the dry bulk commodities industry.
